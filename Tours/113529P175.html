Sunset Felucca boat On The Nile in Cairo

Tours > Cairo > Sunset Felucca boat On The Nile in Cairo

Why choose this tour ?

We are happy to offer The Cairo felucca boat on the River Nile. Our representative will pick you up from your hotel and transfer you to your felucca to start your amazing sailing tour with some activities and amazing sightseeing opportunities, comfort back to your hotel. The Cairo felucca on the Nile tour is available during both nighttime and daytime hours; tourists are welcomed to book this trip for birthday parties, anniversaries, engagements, or any special occasion, the sunrise hours are a favorite for this sailing tour. This tour runs 24 hours per day with multiple departure times, 7 days a week. You will be escorted back to Cairo or Giza in an air conditioned vehicle.
